Smart Fan

A smart fan that can be controlled using a mobile application. The fan can be turned on/off, and the speed can be adjusted using the mobile application. The fan is equipped with sensors to detect the room temperature and humidity, and can automatically adjust the speed based on the room conditions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Publish a research paper on design and development of AIoT circuit of BLDC ceiling fans
  • Done a study on different machine learning algorithms for speed prediction using different parameters and past user preferred speed without disturbing user comfort
  • Designed and developed a retrofittable IoT circuit prototype on veroboard
  • Debug and resolve real time problems of IoT circuits
