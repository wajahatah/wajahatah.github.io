RoboDoc

A doctor assistive robot to isolate patient and doctor in the situation of COVID-19 pandemic. Equipped with sensors for real-time patient vitals monitoring, vision algorithms for facial expression detection, and a dashboard for detailed data visualization, streamlining patient care for medical professionals.

Key Responsibilities

  • Firmware for locomotion and haptic feedback
  • Computer vision algorithm for action recognition, sentimental analysis, and object detection
  • Integrate sensors and camera with microprocessor using BLE and MQTT protocol
